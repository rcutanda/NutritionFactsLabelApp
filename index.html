Nutrition Facts Label App

Study real branded food products
from the USDA FoodData Central database.

1. Download a label analysis checklist in PDF format.

2. Click here to search info for a particular product

3. EAT-Lancet reference plate (the canonical 2500 kcal target) and a personalized version scaled to your daily energy needs

🚩 Red — High (>20% Daily Value or ingredient used in ultra-processed products): nutrient to limit is very high per serving.
⚠️ Orange — Low (<5% Daily Value): beneficial nutrient is very low per serving.
✅ Green: either a beneficial nutrient is high (>20%Daily Value), or a nutrient to limit is low (<5% Daily Value).

Source: NOVA Group 4 (Monteiro et al., 2019, Public Health Nutrition) & FAO/WHO Codex Alimentarius.
